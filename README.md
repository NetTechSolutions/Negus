The Market Opportunity

Salon clients seek Instagram-worthy interiors that convey luxury and hygiene; boutiques require layouts that optimize merchandise display and customer flow; and hospitality businesses need atmospheres that balance comfort, acoustics, and durability. There is a distinct gap in the local market for a designer who understands the specific operational nuances of these industries while delivering world-class finishes.

Our Approach
We offer a full-service design solution tailored to the commercial client. Our methodology is rooted in a deep understanding of the Bulawayo context, considering local climate, material availability, and the unique architectural heritage of the city. Our process includes:

1. Concept Development: We translate your brand identity into a spatial narrative, focusing on color psychology, lighting design, and materiality that resonates with your target demographic.
2. Operational Flow Analysis: For salons and boutiques, we prioritize ergonomic workspaces and logical circulation paths to enhance staff productivity and customer experience. For hospitality, we focus on zoning to manage acoustic privacy and social energy.
3. Procurement & Project Management: Leveraging local supply chains and vetted contractors in Zimbabwe, we manage the entire project lifecycle—from sourcing finishes and furniture to overseeing construction—ensuring strict adherence to timelines and budget.
