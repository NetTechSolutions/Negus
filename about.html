Design is your most powerful business asset.

about

We design commercial spaces that perform.

Negus is a Bulawayo-based interior design practice dedicated to transforming commercial spaces into strategic business assets. Recognizing that the city’s retail and hospitality sectors are the heartbeat of the local economy, we specialize in creating high-impact environments for salons, boutiques, and hospitality venues.

Our work bridges the gap between aesthetic sophistication and functional durability, ensuring that every space not only looks exceptional but also drives operational efficiency and customer loyalty.

market

The physical environment is your brand.

Bulawayo’s commercial landscape is experiencing a renaissance, with a growing demand for distinctive, high-quality spaces that cater to discerning consumers. In the beauty, fashion, and hospitality sectors, the physical environment is no longer a secondary consideration—it is a primary driver of brand perception and revenue.

Salon clients seek Instagram-worthy interiors that convey luxury and hygiene; boutiques require layouts that optimize merchandise display and customer flow; and hospitality businesses need atmospheres that balance comfort, acoustics, and durability. There is a distinct gap in the local market for a designer who understands the specific operational nuances of these industries while delivering world-class finishes.

partner

Invest in your bottom line.

Investing in professional interior design is an investment in your business’s bottom line. By choosing Negus, you gain a partner who is committed to delivering spaces that are:

  • Brand-Centric: Your space becomes a physical manifestation of your brand story.
  • Operationally Sound: Designed to reduce staff fatigue and streamline service delivery.
  • Aesthetically Distinct: Stand out in a competitive market with a unique, memorable environment.
