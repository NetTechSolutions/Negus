studio

We create environments that inspire and elevate.

Negus is a premier Zimbabwean interior architecture, design, renovation, and exhibition solutions firm. Inspired by the word Negus, meaning "King" or "Royalty," our brand embodies elegance, sophistication, innovation, and uncompromising attention to detail.

We specialize in transforming residential, commercial, hospitality, and exhibition spaces. From concept development and space planning to interior renovations and experiential brand environments, we deliver bespoke solutions that combine functionality with timeless aesthetics, tailored exactly to our client's vision.

vision

Africa's benchmark for excellence.

Our vision is to become Africa's benchmark for interior architecture and design excellence by creating transformative spaces that inspire people, strengthen brands, and enrich lifestyles. We believe exceptional spaces are created through careful planning, precision, and thoughtful design execution.

We deliver royal-standard solutions through creativity, innovation, craftsmanship, and client-centered service. Our deep understanding of local markets is complemented by international design trends, ensuring we provide premium outcomes that exceed expectations in functionality, aesthetics, and value.

services

End-to-end design solutions.

We offer comprehensive interior architecture & design, spatial planning, 3D visualization, and material selection. Furthermore, Negus revitalizes existing environments through expertly managed renovations, transforming residential and commercial properties into modern, functional spaces.

Additionally, we create immersive exhibition environments and brand activations. By incorporating custom stands, modular systems, and advanced lighting integration, we help brands stand out and engage audiences powerfully. Throughout all services, we remain committed to sustainable and environmentally conscious design practices.

founder

Mr Shadreck Ngorima

Negus was founded with a singular conviction: that African spaces deserve the same level of design rigour as those in any global city. Mr Ngorima brings a results-driven approach, pursuing the highest standards in creativity, integrity, and client-centricity. We design with purpose, create with excellence, and inspire through innovation.
