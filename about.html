studio

We design commercial spaces that perform.

Negus is a Bulawayo-based interior design practice dedicated to transforming commercial spaces into strategic business assets. We specialize in salons, boutiques, and hospitality venues — the environments where brand experience and business performance intersect.

Our methodology is rooted in the Bulawayo context: local climate, material availability, and the city's architectural heritage inform every decision. From ergonomic station layouts and acoustic zoning to procurement and construction management, we handle the complete project lifecycle — from concept brief to final walkthrough.

market

Bulawayo's commercial renaissance.

Bulawayo's retail and hospitality sectors are experiencing a renaissance, with a growing demand for distinctive, high-quality spaces that serve discerning consumers. Salon clients seek Instagram-worthy interiors that convey luxury and hygiene; boutiques require layouts that maximize merchandise impact and guide customer flow; hospitality businesses need atmospheres that balance comfort, acoustics, and durability.

There is a clear gap in the local market for a designer who understands the operational nuances of these industries while delivering world-class finishes. Negus fills that gap — combining international design sensibility with a deep understanding of what works in Zimbabwe.

process

Full-service, start to finish.

We translate your brand identity into a spatial narrative — centering on color psychology, lighting design, and materiality that resonates with your target clientele. For salons and boutiques we prioritize ergonomic workspaces and logical circulation; for hospitality we focus on acoustic zoning and social energy.

Leveraging local supply chains and vetted contractors across Zimbabwe, we manage every stage of your commercial fit-out — from sourcing finishes and furniture to overseeing construction — ensuring strict adherence to timelines and budget.

founder

Mr Shadreck Ngorima

Negus was founded with a singular conviction: that commercial spaces in Bulawayo deserve the same level of design rigour as those in any global city. Mr Ngorima brings a results-driven approach to every project — measuring success not just in aesthetics but in footfall, dwell time, and client retention for the businesses we serve.
